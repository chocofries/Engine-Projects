Cargo Pirates

By John Bu and Rory More, 2018

Menu:
Up and Down arrows to control, Enter to select menu option.

Game:
Left, Right, Up, Down, to control your helicopter in all 4-directions!
Hold down Spacebar to fire a stream of bullets!
Dodge enemy bullets, and destroy the pirates before they reach your cargo ships!

Win/Lose Screen:
Press Escape to go to menu
If the player won the previous round, the next round's enemies will have more damage, but also give more score.

If you get into the high scores, a dialog will open for you to enter a name!

Debug Window:
Press F1 to open the debug window!
